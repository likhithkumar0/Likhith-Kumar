ABOUT

DWAYNE DOUGLAS JOHNSON also known as 'THE ROCK' born on 2 May 1971 (age 49), he is an American actor, producer, businessman and a retired professional wrestler. Johnson was a college football player at the University of Miami, with whom he won a national championship in 1991. He aspired to have a professional career in football and entered the 1995 NFL Draft, but went undrafted.

In 2004, he left the WWE to pursue an acting career. He returned in 2011 as a part-time performer until 2013, and made sporadic appearances until retiring in 2019.Johnson's first leading film role was as the titular character in The Scorpion King (2002), having previously briefly portrayed the character in The Mummy Returns (2001)
